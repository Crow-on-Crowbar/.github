# Crow on Crowbar

## Team Introduction

**"Crow on Crowbar"** is an independent game development team driven by creativity and passion. The team focuses on building **fun** and **engaging games** through a combination of various technologies and ideas. The developers primarily utilize **different game engines** to create **innovative projects** while continuously learning and evolving.

---

### What does the name **"Crow on Crowbar"** mean?

The team's name symbolizes a **blend of a tool and a bird**, representing the strong and creative mindset of the team.  
- **"Crowbar"** refers to a reliable and sturdy tool, symbolizing the developer's ability to **build** and **solve problems**.  
- **"Crow"** symbolizes **sharp thinking** and **quick reflexes**, reflecting the team's approach of solving problems and pursuing innovative methods throughout the game development process.

---

### Our Approach to Game Development

Every game created by **Crow on Crowbar** prioritizes:
- **Fun**
- **Unique ideas**
- **Focus on user experience**

The team is committed to crafting **memorable** and **enjoyable** games that stand out for their creativity and innovation.

---

**Crow on Crowbar** is more than just a team—it's a vision. A vision to **redefine gaming experiences** with a balance of thoughtful design, technical skill, and unrelenting passion. 🚀
